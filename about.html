Our Story

We at Asharna scented candle, are passionate about creating candles that not only fill your space with captivating fragrances but also prioritize the use of soy wax instead of petroleum wax. We believe in providing you with a clean and eco-friendly candle experience. Our candles are handcrafted with love, using 100% natural soy wax derived from soybean oil. By choosing soy wax, we ensure that our candles burn longer, cleaner, and emit less soot compared to traditional petroleum-based candles. You can enjoy a longer-lasting and healthier candle experience without compromising on the delightful scents. We take pride in our meticulous attention to detail, from selecting premium fragrance oils to hand-pouring each candle with care. Whether you're looking to create a cozy atmosphere, unwind after a long day, or simply add a touch of elegance to your space, our scented soy wax candles are here to elevate your moments. Join us on this aromatic journey and light up your space, relax. and let the enchanting aromas transport you to a world of tranquility.

Our Mission

At Candle Store, our mission is to illuminate spaces with the warmth and tranquility that only a handcrafted candle can offer. We are committed to creating products that evoke a sense of peace, inspire relaxation, and foster moments of mindfulness in an increasingly busy world. Each of our candles is made from high-quality, sustainable materials to ensure not only a beautiful and clean burn but also an eco-friendly experience that respects the planet. We believe that the gentle glow of a candle holds the power to transform ordinary moments into cherished memories, whether it's the flicker during a quiet evening, a special celebration, or a simple reminder to pause and breathe. We are driven by the passion to blend unique fragrances that bring life to any space, transporting our customers to places that comfort the soul. Our team of dedicated artisans pours their heart into each creation, ensuring that every product meets our high standards of craftsmanship and quality. Our mission extends beyond creating great products. We strive to foster connections within our community by supporting local suppliers and sharing our love for artisanal craftsmanship. By choosing our candles, you're not just purchasing a product; you're investing in moments of joy, peace, and shared experiences that last a lifetime.
